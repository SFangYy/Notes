- Basic bullets
	- [/] incomplete (half completed) (- [/])
	- [x] done (- [x])
	- [-] canceled (- [-])
	 - [I] idea (- [I])
	 - [*] Events (- [\*])
- Migration bullets
	 - [>] forward (to Future Log) (- [>])
	 - [<] re-scheduling (reschedule to the next day's Daily Log) (- [<])
- Extended bullets
	- [i] important (- [i])
	- [u] up (- [u])
	- [d] down (- [d])
	- ["] quote (- ["])
	- [l] location (- [l])
